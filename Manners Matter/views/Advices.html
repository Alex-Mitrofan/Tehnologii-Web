List of advices:

  1. In personal texts, anything goes when it comes to emojis.
  2. On social media, serious content warrants fewer emojis due to their cartoonish nature.
  3. Emojis should be used sparingly in semi formal communication.
  4. Using emojis with coworkers depends on the nature of the relationship and the subject of discussion.
  5. If your communication with a client or customer has always centered around business and nothing else, steer clear from emoji use
  6. When communicating with your boss or any higher-up, emoji use is ill-advised.
  7. In video conference mute yourself when not speaking.
  8. Be on time on meetings.
  9. Before joining a video conference ensure your technology works correctly.
  10. In a video conference wear work-appropriate clothing.
  11. In a video conference frame the camera correctly.
  12. In a video conference look into the camera.
  13. In a conference or interview pay attention.
